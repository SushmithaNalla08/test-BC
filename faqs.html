❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Olympianz

🤔 General Questions

What is Olympianz?

Olympianz is a blockchain-based certificate verification system that provides secure, transparent, and immutable credential management. It allows educational institutions to issue certificates, students to own their digital credentials, and anyone to verify their authenticity instantly.

How does blockchain make certificates secure?

Blockchain creates an immutable ledger of all certificates. Once issued, a certificate cannot be changed, deleted, or forged. Every transaction is cryptographically verified and recorded permanently, making fraud virtually impossible.

How much does it cost to use Olympianz?

Our platform offers flexible pricing models. Students and verifiers can use the platform for free. Institutions can choose from various subscription plans based on their needs. Contact our sales team for custom enterprise solutions.

Is my data safe and private?

Yes. We use military-grade encryption for all data. Your personal information is only visible to you and the institutions you authorize. Blockchain records are transparent but anonymized with wallet addresses.

🎓 For Students

How do I access my certificates?

Simply log in to your student dashboard using your wallet credentials. All certificates issued to you will appear there. You can view, download, or share them instantly.

Can I download my certificates?

Yes! You can download certificates as PDF files anytime from your dashboard. You can also share a verification link with employers or other institutions.

What is a wallet address?

A wallet address is your unique identifier on the blockchain, similar to a bank account number. It's generated when you connect with MetaMask or another Web3 wallet. This is where your certificates are recorded.

How do I set up a wallet?

Download MetaMask from metamask.io, create an account, and follow the setup instructions. Once created, you'll have your wallet address ready to use with Olympianz.

👨‍💼 For Administrators

How do I issue a certificate?

Log into the Admin Dashboard, fill in the student's details, course information, and upload the certificate PDF. Click "Issue Certificate" and it will be recorded permanently on the blockchain.

Can I revoke a certificate?

Yes, administrators can revoke certificates if needed. Once revoked, the certificate will be marked as invalid and will fail verification. This action is recorded on the blockchain.

How do I batch issue certificates?

You can upload a CSV file with student details to issue multiple certificates at once. This saves time and ensures consistency across large issuances.

What reports are available?

The admin panel provides detailed reports on issued, verified, and revoked certificates, as well as user activity logs and blockchain transactions.

⚙️ Technical Questions

Which blockchain does Olympianz use?

Olympianz uses Ethereum for certificate recording and verification. Ethereum provides security, transparency, and widespread adoption in the blockchain ecosystem.

Do I need to pay gas fees?

When using Ganache (local blockchain) for testing, there are no real fees. On the main Ethereum network, small gas fees apply for certificate issuance, which institutions typically cover.

Is the platform scalable?

Yes. Olympianz is designed to scale from small institutions to large enterprises. We're also exploring Layer 2 solutions for even faster and cheaper transactions.

Can I integrate Olympianz with my system?

Yes. We provide REST API and SDKs for integration. Our documentation includes examples for common platforms and frameworks.
